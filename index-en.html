About me


Hi, my name is Olivier Casalta, I am 37. I live in Christchurch New Zealand and love this place because of its high quality of life. I like playing music and doing all sort of outdoors sports like spearfishing, climbing, mountain biking, etc.


I originaly come from Montpellier in France where I grew up and studied. I graduated with my master's degree in Mechatronics 11 years ago and went to work abroad for major construction projects as a supervisor.


Job Search


I am looking for a new challenge which requires technical and managerial skills for projects in France. My international on-site experience allows me to respond to any type of emergency situation and to solve problems that engage responsibility, authority and skills.


In recent years, I have mainly worked abroad as a project engineer in construction and commissioning, for new power plants in Algeria, France, Estonia and for infrastructures in New Zealand. These large projects have considerably broadened my technical skills in the electromechanical industry and strengthened my motivation to lead groups. All of these experiences, both in the field and remotely, have constantly transformed the way I work and improved my ability to manage teams.


My current position at an energy monitoring innovation company, leading EMS in New Zealand's power infrastructures, has given me an excellent approach to tackling power transmission and distribution issues.
